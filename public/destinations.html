Top Destinations

Morocco offers a stunning variety of destinations, from the imperial cities to the desert dunes and coastal towns.

Marrakech

The Red City is famous for its bustling souks, palaces, and the Jemaa el-Fnaa square.

Best time to visit: Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November).

Fez

Home to the world's oldest university and a massive, labyrinthine medina.

Best time to visit: Spring and Autumn to avoid extreme heat.

Chefchaouen

The Blue Pearl of Morocco, nestled in the Rif Mountains, known for its blue-washed buildings.

Best time to visit: Spring and early Summer.

Casablanca

Morocco's economic hub and home to the magnificent Hassan II Mosque.

Travel Tip: A great entry point with the country's largest international airport.

Merzouga

The gateway to the Erg Chebbi dunes in the Sahara Desert.

Best time to visit: October to April for comfortable temperatures.

Essaouira

A windy coastal city known for its historic ramparts and fresh seafood.

Best time to visit: Year-round, but can be very windy in summer.
