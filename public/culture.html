Moroccan Culture

A Rich Tapestry of Traditions, Arts, and Hospitality

Customs & Traditions

Hospitality & Mint Tea

Offering mint tea is a sign of friendship and hospitality. It's often served with pastries and poured from a height.

Music & Festivals

From Gnaoua in Essaouira to the Fes Festival of World Sacred Music, Morocco is alive with rhythm.

Artisan Crafts

Moroccan artisans are masters of weaving, pottery, leatherwork, and zellige (tilework).

The Hammam

The traditional steam bath is a vital part of Moroccan life, offering relaxation and social connection.
