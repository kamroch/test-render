Moroccan Beaches

Sun, Sand, and Surf on the Atlantic and Mediterranean

Coastal Paradises

Agadir

Known for its wide crescent beach and resort atmosphere. Perfect for sun-seekers and families.

Essaouira

A historic port city with a beautiful beach popular for kitesurfing and windsurfing.

Taghazout

Morocco's premier surfing destination, offering laid-back vibes and world-class waves.

Tangier

Where the Mediterranean meets the Atlantic, offering unique city beaches and nearby coves.
