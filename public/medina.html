Historic Medinas

Step Back in Time in Morocco's Ancient Walled Cities

A Journey Through History

Fez el-Bali

The largest car-free urban area in the world, a UNESCO World Heritage site filled with mosques, madrasas, and tanneries.

Marrakech Medina

A sensory overload of sights, sounds, and smells. Explore the souks, the Koutoubia Mosque, and the Bahia Palace.

Tetouan

Known for its Andalusian influence and whitewashed buildings, offering a unique blend of Arab and Spanish culture.

Meknes

One of the four Imperial cities, featuring the impressive Bab Mansour gate and the Mausoleum of Moulay Ismail.
